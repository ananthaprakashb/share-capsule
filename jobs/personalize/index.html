Resume-based matching

Turn your résumé into better job searches.

Upload a résumé, review the skills and role keywords found in it, and compare them with current Share Capsule jobs and official employer searches.

Processed locally in your browser — your résumé is not uploaded

1. Add your résumé

Supported formats: PDF, DOCX and TXT. Scanned image-only PDFs may not contain extractable text.

2. Review search keywords

Remove weak terms or add role titles, tools, licenses and skills that matter to you.

3. Matching jobs

Engineering openings are scored individually. Truck, warehouse and cooking results link to official employer searches that match your résumé keywords.
